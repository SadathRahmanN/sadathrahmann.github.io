About Me

Developer & Designer

My name is Sadath Rahman and I recently completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I have a passion for designing and building websites that not only look great but also function well. As a web designer, I enjoy creating visually appealing layouts and selecting color schemes that complement the content of each page. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow in my field. Whether I'm working on a small personal project or a large-scale website for a client, I am committed to delivering high-quality results that exceed expectations.
